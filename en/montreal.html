Welcome to the Morocco Property Expo, the leading real estate investment event

Montreal will host the March 2024 road show Morocco Expo with the participation of more than 10,000 visitors composed mainly of MRE, executives and professionals and local interested more than ever to acquire housing and lots of land in Morocco

Your opportunity to discover real estate projects in Morocco.

89% of Moroccans living in Saudi Arabia consider that the purchase of real estate property in Morocco is very important.

Whether for the purchase of a principal residence, a second home or for an investment project, your visit will provide with an insight of different opportunities.

During 3 days, you will be able to exchange with real estate professionals from Morocco, obtain personalized advises and offers for your investment project.

Over an area of over 2000 m2, the road show will also bring together promoters with projects in the different regions of the kingdom in addition to spaces for crafts and the Moroccan lifestyle. Multiple conferences and debates will be led by industry professionals who will take stock of major market trends, financing, new tax and regulatory provisions.

The road show will be open for free with a very rich schedule planned every day.
